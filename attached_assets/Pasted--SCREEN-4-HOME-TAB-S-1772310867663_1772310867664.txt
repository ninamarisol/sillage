--- HOME: MY VAULT ---
Header: user's archetype name in small gold text above their username.
Subtext: "14 bottles in your vault"

THE VAULT DISPLAY — this is the hero visual of the entire app:
A lit glass display shelf unit. Dark background. 3 horizontal shelves rendered 
in frosted glass (semi-transparent white border, glowing bottom edge using 
box-shadow in warm white/gold). Each shelf holds up to 5 bottles.

Each perfume bottle is a unique SVG rendered in JSX. Five distinct silhouettes:
  A: Tall rectangular art deco (YSL Libre style)
  B: Short round flacon (Chanel No.5 style)
  C: Geometric angular faceted (Creed/Killian style)
  D: Wide oval (Lancôme La Vie est Belle style)
  E: Flat wide square (Tom Ford style)

Each bottle has:
  - Semi-transparent glass body with rgba fill + subtle gradient
  - Colored liquid fill visible inside (fill level reflects % full):
    amber = orientals, pale gold = florals, icy blue = aquatics, 
    deep burgundy = dark/oud, blush pink = light florals, clear = fresh
  - Gold or silver cap SVG on top
  - Tiny serif label on bottle body with fragrance name
  - Thin diagonal white gradient "shine" streak across the body
  - Soft drop shadow beneath bottle on the glass shelf surface (reflection effect)

Shelf ambiance:
  - Warm light appears to come from above, casting downward shadows
  - Very subtle slow-drifting dust/bokeh particles in background (pure CSS animation)
  - Empty slots show faint "+" dotted outline

Bottle interactions:
  - Hover/tap: bottle lifts (translateY -10px, scale 1.05), gold glow ring appears 
    around base, floating info card shows: name, house, fill %, AI match score, 
    wear count
  - Tap info card: opens bottom drawer with full details — wear log timeline, 
    "Log a Wear" button (triggers spray particle animation from bottle top), 
    fill level slider, "List on Exchange" button, "Add to wishlist" button

STATE MANAGEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════
Use React useState and useContext to manage:
  - currentScreen (splash / login / quiz / app)
  - activeTab (home / explore / profile)
  - homeSubTab (vault / log / totry)
  - exploreSubTab (feed / exchange)
  - isSubscribed (boolean, default false — toggle button somewhere in Exchange 
    to simulate subscribing, for demo purposes)
  - userArchetype (set after quiz, used across all screens)
  - quizStep (0-7 for quiz progress)
  - vaultBottles (array of bottle objects)
  - toTryList (array)
  - wearLog (array)
